A Quick Look at the Wrap of the Day Concept

The Wrap of the Day offers a different wrap at a special price each day of the week, keeping things fresh and exciting for customers. This rotating menu provides variety while ensuring affordability, making it a go-to choice for lunch, dinner, or a satisfying snack.

What’s New for 2025?

1. Spicy Sriracha Chicken Wrap

The Spicy Sriracha Chicken Wrap brings a fiery kick with crispy chicken, fresh lettuce, and a zesty sriracha mayo. It’s perfect for those who crave heat with every bite.

2. Vegan Mediterranean Delight

For plant-based eaters, the Vegan Mediterranean Delight is a game-changer. Featuring falafel, hummus, crunchy veggies, and a drizzle of tahini, this wrap offers a fresh and wholesome option.

3. Crispy BBQ Bacon Wrap

This new addition is all about smoky flavors and crispy textures. The Crispy BBQ Bacon Wrap combines crispy chicken, bacon, lettuce, and a sweet and smoky BBQ sauce for a satisfying crunch.

Fan Favorites That Are Here to Stay

1. Garlic Mayo Chicken Wrap

The Garlic Mayo Chicken Wrap remains a top pick for its creamy garlic mayo, tender chicken, and fresh veggies. It’s a consistent winner for those who love rich, savory flavors.

2. Sweet Chili Chicken Wrap

With its perfect balance of sweet and spicy, the Sweet Chili Chicken Wrap continues to be a staple. The tangy sauce pairs beautifully with the crispy chicken and fresh lettuce.

3. Fiery Buffalo Chicken Wrap

Buffalo sauce lovers will be pleased to see this wrap sticking around. It offers a tangy and slightly spicy experience, making it a popular choice for those who like their wraps with a bit of a punch.
